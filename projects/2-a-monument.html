2: a monument

2023
  • Sculpture Installation
  • a study from two collge beds
  • Exhibited at Room 234B Gallery, Stanford University
  • press

After my two roommates in College left our apartment, I was compelled to make their room into a monument of their presence there. I deconstructed their beds and made sculptures out of them, each representing one of the past-tenants of the room. I covered the floor with white paper to demarcate that the room has ceased to be just a room and is now a temple, or a cloud, or a memory.
